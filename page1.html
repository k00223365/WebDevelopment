For the last number of years I have been writing, arranging and self-recording music under the pseudonym, The Near Future! A project that combines various elements and styles such as Funk, Jazz, Fusion, Trip-Hop, Rock and Experimental sounds. Using music production software such as, Steinberg's 'Cubase', Avid's 'Sibelius 7' & Wave's 'GTR 3.5'. To date I have self released three E.P.'s....

'Stop, Start, Rewind' E.P. (2016)

'Atoms for Henrietta (Spinning Carousels In Her Mind)' E.P. (2014)

'A Little Understanding' E.P. (2010)
